Heart Drop System

Gain extra hearts by defeating mobs and players. Hearts boost your maximum health, giving you an edge in survival and combat. Stay alive, grow stronger, and dominate the battlefield!

  • Collect heart drops from players
  • Increase your maximum health by acquiring more hearts
  • Risk your hearts in battles

Revive Beacon

Revive Beacons allow players to safely store or recover hearts without engaging in combat. Crafted with special materials, they provide a secure way to preserve your strength and assist others when needed.

  • Safely store hearts using a Revive Beacon
  • Recover lost hearts without risking battle
  • Craft and place Revive Beacons strategically

Custom Heart Crafting

Master the art of heart forging! Use rare materials gathered from your adventures to craft custom hearts, expanding your life force and turning the tide of every battle in your favor.

  • Forge powerful custom hearts from rare drops
  • Expand your maximum health and resilience
  • Craft to unlock special rewards and honors

Essential Commands

/help - get help /coins - check your coins /coins give [player] [amount] - give coins to a player /vaults - check your vaults /chatcolor - change your chat color (for ranked players) /eglow - change your glow color (for ranked players) /shop - open the shop /sell - sell items to the shop /store - open the store /discord - join the discord server /vote - vote for the server /socials - join the social media /balance - check your balance /balance give [player] [amount] - give balance to a player /baltop - check the balance leaderboard /coinshop - open the coin shop

Pro Tips & Strategies

Survival Basics

  • Always keep spare hearts in storage
  • Build secure bases with multiple layers
  • Use ender chests for valuable items
  • Form alliances for protection

Combat Strategy

  • Invest in quality armor and weapons
  • Practice PvP in safe zones
  • Learn to use potions effectively
  • Master the art of escape

Heart Economy Strategy

  • Keep track of heart values and scarcity
  • Secure your strength by building a reserve of hearts
  • Adapt your playstyle to the shifting heart economy
  • Trade wisely - every heart could change your fate

Rules & Guidelines

Combat Rules

  • No combat logging (logging out during fights)
  • No using glitches or exploits
  • Respect safe zones and protected areas
  • No creating teams in battlegrounds
  • Report hackers to staff immediately

Trading Rules

  • All trades must use official trading systems
  • No scamming or false advertising
  • Report any bugs or exploits to staff
  • No real-money trading
  • Keep screenshots of important trades

Base Building Rules

  • No building in spawn protection
  • Respect world boundaries
  • No excessive redstone lag machines
  • Keep bases 1000 blocks from spawn
  • Report grief attempts to staff

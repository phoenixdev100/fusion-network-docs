Rules & Guidelines

Combat Rules

  • No combat logging (logging out during fights)
  • No teaming in FFA areas
  • No using glitches or exploits
  • Respect arena boundaries
  • Report hackers to staff immediately

Kit Rules

  • Use only provided kits
  • No modifying kit items
  • Report any kit-related bugs
  • No sharing kit items
  • Reset kit after each match

Tournament Rules

  • Be on time for matches
  • Follow staff instructions
  • No unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Matches are final
  • Prize distribution is non-negotiable

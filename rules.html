Warning System

Progressive discipline system

  • Level 1: Verbal warning and reminder of rules
  • Level 2: 1-hour mute or 24-hour temporary ban
  • Level 3: 7-day ban from all server activities
  • Level 4: 30-day ban with appeal option
  • Severe: Permanent ban from the server
üìù

Appeal Process

How to appeal your punishment

  • Join our Discord server and go to #appeal-requests
  • Fill out the appeal form with your Ban ID
  • Provide a detailed explanation of the situation
  • Include any evidence supporting your case
  • Wait 24-48 hours for staff review
  • Follow up in #appeal-status channel

Reporting System

How to report rule violations

  • Use /report [player] [reason] in-game
  • Take screenshots or record evidence
  • Submit detailed reports on Discord
  • Include time, date, and server
  • List any witnesses present
  • Maintain confidentiality during investigation

Application Process

Step 1: Initial Application

Submit your application through our online form. Include your experience, skills, and why you want to join our staff team. Be honest and detailed in your responses.

Step 2: Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for a Discord voice interview. We'll discuss your application, assess your knowledge, and evaluate how you handle various scenarios.

Step 3: Training Period

Successful applicants undergo a comprehensive training program. Learn our policies, commands, and best practices while working alongside experienced staff members.

Step 4: Final Review

After completing training, your performance will be reviewed. Upon passing, you'll become an official member of our staff team with full responsibilities.
